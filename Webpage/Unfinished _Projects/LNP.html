Welcome to the Late NIght Pizza webpage!

Here I'll be showcasing the current progress I've made on an up and coming video game: Late Night Pizza. I'm in a team with several artists, though I am the only programmer for the entire project. That means I'm handling everything not related to the graphics! It's a tall order, I know! I joined the project as its programmer in July 2024. In joining the project, not only have I had to learn a whole new programming language to work with Godot Utility Engine, but I had to learn how to program video games in general. This is the first video game I've ever programmed for, so each step in development is a learning milestone for me.

As of December 2024, here's all the progress I've made:

  • Rigged player body and controls to move in a three-dimensional plane
  • Uploaded scenery models and applied collisions to the scene to give the player character areas to traverse
